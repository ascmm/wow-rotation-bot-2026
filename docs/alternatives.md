## Why SimC accuracy matters

SimulationCraft is the gold standard for WoW theorycrafting. It models the game's combat system mathematically and generates Action Priority Lists (APLs) that represent the theoretical optimum rotation for each spec.

A tool that executes real SimC APLs will always outperform one that approximates them. The gap compounds over a raid night or M+ season.

- **1–13% below SimC max** (AEMIS) = strong player performance, undetectable parse range
- **15–40% below SimC max** (competitors) = visible underperformance, especially on complex specs
